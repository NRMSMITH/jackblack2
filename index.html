Acting Career


Thomas Jacob "Jack" Black has appeared in over 70 films. Starting as Roger Davis in Bob Roberts(1992) to Adult Stanley in Apollo 10 1/2: A Space Age Childhood (2022), his career has spanned decades and touched the hearts of many.

Fun facts:

  • At the age of 13, he was cast in the advert for Atari's Activision Pitfall.
  • In School of Rock (2004), Black did the stage dive at the end. To ensure his safety, a lot of stuntmen were placed to help catch him if he were to fall or be dropped.

Music Career


Jack Black is a co-founder and member of Tenacious D, a comedy rock duo, alongside Kyle Gass. Together, Black and Gass have written and produced 4 albums and have won two awards:

  • 2021: Kerrang! Awards: Kerrang! Service to Rock
  • 2015: Grammy Award: Best Metal Performance for "The Last in Line"
